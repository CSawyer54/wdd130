    How To Prepare For Whitewater Rafting:

  • Bring lunch.
  • Wear water shoes.
  • Come 30 minutes early.
  • Bring sunscreen and a sunshirt.
      • *If you have any more questions, contact your rafting guide for more ways to prepare.
